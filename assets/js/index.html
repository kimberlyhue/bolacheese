Chicken Cheese Bread

Ingredients (Baked Assado)
Tapioca Flour - Eggs - Salt - Cheese - Oil - Milk - Chicken

How to bake
Take out the cheese bread from the freezer, let it chill for 30 minutes outside. Heat the oven at 450°F. When it's done, put all the cheese bread you want in the oven between 30 and 45 minutes, keep checking until it starts getting a golden color. You made it! Be careful, it might be very hot.

Chicken Coxinha

Ingredients (Fried Frito)
Wheat Flour - Water - Milk - Bouillon Chicken - Butter - Chicken

How to fry
Take out the Coxinhas from the freezer, let it chill for 30 minutes outside. Heat a heavy-bottomed pot with enough oil to cover the coxinhas. Heat the oil to 360 F. Fry them in batches until deep golden brown.

More

How to fry in the air fryer
Take out the Kibes from the freezer, let it chill for 30 minutes outside. Apply a little bit of oil on the air fryer grill and over the Kibes. Put all the Kibes you want in the air fryer between 20 and 25 minutes.

How to bake
Take out the Kibes from the freezer, let it chill for 30 minutes outside. Heat the oven at 450°F. When it's done, put all the Kibes you want in the oven between 30 and 45 minutes, keep checking until it starts getting a darker color. You made it! Be careful, it might be very hot.
